1. Video to Audio (Best Quality):
    ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vn -acodec pcm_s16le -ar 48000 -ac 2 output.wav

    -vn remove video
    pcm_s16le uncompressed WAV (maximum quality)
    -ar 48000 48 kHz audio sampling
    -ac 2 stereo

2. Video to Audio (Best Storage):
    ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vn -acodec libmp3lame -b:a 96k output.mp3

    libmp3lame MP3 codec
    -b:a 96k low but clear bitrate
    Ideal for saving storage

4. Image Converter (Best Storage):
    convert input.jpg -quality 40 output.jpg

    Good balance of clarity + small size

    convert input.png -quality 30 output.webp

5. Video Converter (Best Quality):
    ffmpeg -i input.mkv -c:v libx264 -preset veryslow -crf 15 -c:a copy output.mp4

    libx264 stable H.264 encoder
    veryslow best compression efficiency
    crf 15 near-lossless
    copy keeps original audio

6. Video Converter (Best Storage):
    ffmpeg -i input.mkv -c:v libx265 -preset medium -crf 28 -c:a aac -b:a 96k output.mp4
     
    libx265 very efficient HEVC codec       
    crf 28 lightweight
    aac 96k small audio bitrate

7. Video Compression (Best Quality):
    ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -c:v libx264 -crf 18 -preset slow -c:a copy output.mp4

    libx265 very efficient HEVC codec
    crf 28 lightweight
    aac 96k small audio bitrate

8. Video Compression (Best Storage):
    ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -c:v libx265 -crf 30 -preset medium -c:a aac -b:a 64k output.mp4

    HEVC shrinks dramatically
    crf 30 small size
    64k audio bitrate
